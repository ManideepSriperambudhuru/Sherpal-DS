Summary

Your steady and balanced approach suits a sustainable SAT journey. Your math precision and thoughtful reading skills are clear strengths, while your tendency to avoid discomfort may slow progress in tougher areas. Focusing on manageable challenges will help you break through plateaus without disrupting your calm. This plan respects your need for harmony while encouraging targeted growth.

Reading Writing Strategies

  1. Build skill in Craft and Structure by engaging with complex sentence relationships through brief, focused exercises that invite reflection rather than pressure.
  2. Refine your strengths in Information and Ideas by practicing summarizing passages aloud or in writing to deepen comprehension and confidence.
  3. Maintain momentum in Standard English Conventions by using gamified grammar apps or quizzes that reward steady progress and align with your preference for gradual growth.

Math Strategies

  1. Focus on Advanced Math by breaking down challenging problems into smaller steps, allowing you to approach complexity without feeling overwhelmed.
  2. Sharpen your Algebra skills by reviewing problem types where you feel less confident, using spaced repetition to reinforce concepts steadily.
  3. Sustain your confidence in Problem-Solving and Data Analysis through timed practice sets that simulate test conditions while keeping stress manageable.
